My name is Nikita and currently I am an international student at Michigan State University. I was born and raised in Moscow, Russia; first 11 years of my education I completed in a gymnasium. All of these years Mathematics was like a major for me. I performed really well in it and kept enjoying it from the very beginning. I participated in many mathematical Olympiads and won a number of them. Mathematics was the only one prior subject for me until the very last grades in my high school.

I decided that plain Mathematics is not for me and attempted to find a field in which I would be able to apply my mathematical skills. My choice fell on Economics, on its part that studies various financial processes, which may be described and analyzed by Mathematics. This desire to learn Economics has driven me to America, to Michigan State University, the place where I will be able to combine Mathematics and Economics.
